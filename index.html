About NEXGEN Explorers

NEXGEN Explorers

NEXGEN Explorers is a student-led technical club committed to fostering innovation, technical excellence, and professional development. The club provides a platform for students to explore emerging technologies, enhance practical skills, and interact with industry experts.

Through workshops, industry sessions, competitions, JAM activities, and technical presentations, we empower students to become future-ready professionals and leaders.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To create a dynamic platform where students can explore emerging technologies, develop professional skills, and become future-ready innovators and leaders.


Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academics and industry through technical workshops, expert sessions, competitions, and collaborative learning opportunities.

Events

📊 Tableau Data Visualization Workshop

The Tableau Workshop provided students with hands-on experience in data visualization, dashboard creation, and business analytics. Participants learned how to transform raw data into meaningful insights using interactive charts and reports.

💻 Adobe Experience Manager (AEM)

This industry-focused session introduced students to Adobe Experience Manager, covering content management, digital asset management, workflow automation, and enterprise-level web solutions used by global companies.

🎤 Just A Minute (JAM)

The JAM Session helped students improve public speaking, communication, confidence, spontaneity, and critical thinking through one-minute impromptu speeches on various topics.

📑 PPT on No-Code & Low-Code Development

Students presented on modern No-Code and Low-Code platforms, exploring how applications can be developed rapidly with minimal coding. The activity enhanced research, presentation, and technical awareness skills.
